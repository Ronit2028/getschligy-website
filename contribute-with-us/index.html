Take a Step Beyond CSR With Social Value Investing

Getscholify is a suppoeting plateform revolutionizing the way how education is delivered to the minorities. The design of this plateform enebles Corporates and Colleges to help the needy meritorious students collectively. The monitory contribution of Corporate/Individual's combined with the scholarship assistance of Colleges supports the dream education of students in need.More than 20 lakh India students quits education after hight school. The main reason behind this is insufficient family funds to support their education. The pandemic in 2020 has made situation even worse, million of dreams shattered. Getscholify is helping these students with the funds donated by Individuals/corporates so that their journey to success never stops.

Advantages of contibuting with

Contribution directly to Institution

Funds which are dedicated by individuals/corporates are transferred directly to the institution chosen by the student to study.

Full contribution transfer

The amount contributed for the education of student is transferred in full without any hidden charges

Transparency

we maintain full transparency with our donors, no hidden requirements or obligations.

Track / Inquire

Donors can track and inquire about their contributions at any time. They can see and get in touch with the student whom they have helped.

Wide scope

The donations made on our platform has a wider scope that means you can donate for students in any part of the country.

Collaborative

The contributions made on Getscholify is of collaborative nature, some expenses of education is covered by colleges also.
