ENGINEERING

Programs Offered

M.Tech Civil Engineering

Master of Technology or MTech Civil Engineering is a 2 year postgraduate technical program which deals with designing, supervision and building of roads, bridges, tunnels, buildings, airports, dams, sewage systems, ports etc.

M.Tech Electrical Engineering

M.Tech. in Electrical Engineering is a 2- year full- time course divided into 4 semesters involving an advanced study of electrical and electronics engineering, computers, and communication, besides that of electronics, electricity, transmission, and generation.

M.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering(Part Time)

To upgrade the skill of the working engineers by induction of new technologies available and developed through years of academic research and development which results in improved quality and profitability of the company.

M.Tech Civil (Construction Technology & Management)

Master of Technology (MTech) Construction Technology is a 2 year post graduate degree in the field of Construction Technology. The course is covered in the semester system, having 4 semesters in total.

M.Tech Civil (Geotechnology Engineering)

M.Tech. in Geotechnical Engineering is a 2-year full-time post-graduate course divided across 4 semesters. This branch of engineering involves the application of principles of soil mechanics and rock mechanics for investigation and study of subsurface materials and conditions.

M.Tech Mechanical Engineering

M. Tech in Mechanical Engineering is a postgraduate course which offers students the combined study of mechanics, kinematics etc which will be used for construction purpose and in the installation of tools and machines.

B.Tech.- Mechanical Engineering

BTech Mechanical Engineering is a 4 year undergraduate engineering degree course. This course prepares the students to become Mechanical Engineers. The objective of this course is to prepare students to apply the principles of mechanical engineering for designing, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems

B.Tech - Computer Science and Engineering

BTech CSE is a 4 year UG course that studies practical and theoretical knowledge of computer hardware and software. This course lays emphasis on the basics of computer programming and networking while also comprising a plethora of topics.

BCA- Bachelor of Computer Application

BCA is a three year undergraduate degree programme for candidates wishing to delve into the world of Computer languages. One of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology, the course gives you an insight into the world of computers and its applications.

B.Tech. Electronics and Communication Engineering

B. Tech ECE (Electronics and Communications Engineering) is an undergraduate program which is of a total of 4 years duration, divided into 8 semesters. The candidates who have passed 12th or any equivalent degree in any Science stream can pursue a course in B. Tech ECE (Electronics and Communications Engineering).

B.Tech. Electrical Engineering

Tech in Electrical Engineering is an undergraduate program of 4 years that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, electromagnetism for the development & maintenance of electrical and electronics equipment.

B.Tech. Civil Engineering

BTech Civil Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program specializing in the design, planning, and construction of buildings. B.Tech Civil Engineering Syllabus in renowned colleges like VIT Vellore, Manav Rachna includes new age topics like material sciences, sustainable energy, smart city technology, and alike along with core Btech civil engineering subjects.

B.Tech. architecture Engineering

BTech Architecture Engineering is an undergraduate program of 4-year duration. The course focuses on teaching the fundamentals of design and construction of buildings.

B.Tech Mining Engineering

Mining engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the practices, theories, technology, science and application of extraction and processing of mineral resources. The study also includes extraction of rare elements and metals.

B.Tech. Printing and Packaging

BTech printing, graphics and packaging, full form Bachelor of technology in printing, graphics and packaging is an undergraduate course of 4 years duration. The course deals with various machines and tools involved in printing, designing graphics and packaging that have day to day applications in media, news, magazines, and printing press.

B.Tech.(Artificial Intelligence)

B. Tech in Computer Science (Artificial Learning and Machine Learning) is an undergraduate programme with advanced learning solutions imparting knowledge of advanced innovations like machine learning, often called deep learning and artificial intelligence.

B.Tech. (Cyber Security)

BTech Cyber Security is a 4 years duration undergraduate degree which emphasizes comprehensive knowledge of Cyber Crime, Network Security, Data structures, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Cryptography, Intrusion Detection and Prevention Systems etc.

B.Tech.(Data Science)

BTech Data Science is a 4 year undergraduate level course that deals with the use of various tools and techniques of science to extract relevant data from a large volume of data available.

B.Tech IT/B.Tech. IT LEET

B. Tech LEET refers to the lateral entry in any B.Tech program. Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Mechanical Engineering, and Electronics & Communication Engineering. Taking admission in any B.Tech program through B.Tech LEET ensures that the candidate is admitted directly to the 2nd Year (3rd semester BTech program).

B.Sc Textile Design

BSc in Textile Design is a 3-year full time undergraduate degree which is designed to offer students thorough knowledge of the formation and utilisation of numerous textiles.

Diploma in Fire safety

Diploma in Fire and Safety Management is a full-time diploma course, the period of this course can vary from 1 to 2 years (usually one). It is an open course that helps students to get better job opportunities and promotion in their field as fire and safety officer, health and safety manager, in charge manager.

Diploma/Certificate in Graphic

Diploma in Graphic Design is a diploma level Animation, Multimedia and Gaming programme that has a duration of 1 year. The course aims at developing the art or skill of combining pictures and text which helps students develop a creative approach and practical skills.

Designing & Web Development

The concept of web design and development has been around for about as long as websites have existed. It used to have a much simpler definition because website creation used to be a much simpler process.
